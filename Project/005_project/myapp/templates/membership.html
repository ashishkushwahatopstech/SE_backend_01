FAQs

Can I freeze my membership? +
Yes! You can freeze your membership for up to 60 days per year on Forge Pro and Elite plans. Visit the front desk or use the member portal.
Is there a joining fee? +
No joining fees, no hidden costs. The price you see is what you pay.
Can I bring a guest? +
Forge Pro members get 2 guest passes/month. Elite members have unlimited guest passes. Starter members can purchase day passes at ₹299.
What happens if I miss a PT session? +
Sessions cancelled 24 hours in advance can be rescheduled. Last-minute cancellations may forfeit the session.
Do you offer student or corporate discounts? +
Yes! 15% off for students (with valid ID) and corporate group rates for 5+ employees. Contact us for details.
